Build your dream magical village while solving charming puzzles!

Step into the enchanted world of Jellyvale, where every puzzle you solve helps you create a fairy tale village of your dreams. From hosting teddy bear tea parties to building frog prince ponds and pumpkin carriages, the possibilities are endless.

Meet delightful characters like the Charming Prince, Red Riding Hood, a cozy witch, and even a weightlifting Gingerbread Man who will guide you through exciting story-driven quests.

Advantages

  • Charming Storyline: 

    Experience engaging fairy tale quests that bring the village to life.

  • Beautiful Visuals: 

    Explore vibrant, fantastical designs and magical decorations.

  • Dynamic Gameplay: 

    Solve puzzles and unlock new areas and characters.

  • Frequent Updates: 

    Enjoy fresh content with each release, including levels and storylines.

  • Community Events: 

    Participate in special seasonal events to earn exclusive rewards.

Benefits

  • Enchanting Soundtrack and Effects:

    Immerse yourself in the game’s soothing yet playful music, paired with satisfying sound effects that make every match, combo, and explosion even more enjoyable and rewarding.

  • Charming and Vibrant Visuals:
