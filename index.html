About us

FAQ

  • How do I start playing?
    Download Sweetie Legends from Google Play, complete the tutorial, and begin matching candies to progress through levels.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes, Sweetie Legends supports offline gameplay, so you can enjoy it anywhere, anytime.
  • Are there in-app purchases?
    Optional in-app purchases are available for boosters, extra lives, and exclusive rewards.
  • What are pet abilities?
    Each pet has unique skills that can clear obstacles, crush candies, and help you progress faster.
  • How do I upgrade my pets?
    Collect special items during levels to enhance your pets' abilities and unlock advanced powers for tougher puzzles.
  • Are there seasonal events?
    Absolutely! Sweetie Legends frequently introduces themed events with exciting levels and unique rewards.
  • What is the city-building feature?
    Use coins earned from levels to construct and upgrade your in-game city, making it uniquely yours!
